How to Solve Today’s Wordle Step by Step

This is the “information game” playbook—optimized for puzzles where vowel placement is the main fork and doubles aren’t a distraction.

  1. Guess 1 — coverage: Pick a starter with five unique letters, at least two vowels, and high-frequency consonants. Your goal is a wide net, not a lucky solve.
  2. Guess 2 — separate hypotheses: If you have multiple vowels “in the word” but poorly placed, choose a guess that forces them into different slots—even if the word isn’t your favorite aesthetically.
