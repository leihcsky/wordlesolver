Common Wordle Patterns

Many 5-letter words follow specific structures. Recognizing these can speed up your solving process:

  • Consonant Clusters: Pairs like ST, TR, GR, or BL often start words.
  • Vowel Placement: Vowels (A, E, I, O, U) often appear in the 2nd or 3rd position.
  • Ending Letters: Common endings include -ER, -ED, -TY, and -CH.
