Hints for Wordle #1658

The answer is PROOF.

Definition: Evidence or argument establishing or helping to establish a fact or the truth of a statement.

Strategy & Analysis

Today's word has a double vowel (OO) which can sometimes be tricky if you don't guess it early.

The starting letter P and ending letter F are key anchors here.

Strategic Tip: Words with double vowels like 'OO' or 'EE' are common. Don't forget to test for them if you have a single vowel confirmed in the middle.
