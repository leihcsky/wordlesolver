The answer is FIERY.

Strategy & Analysis

FIERY uses fairly common letters but can be tricky because of the less common F and Y and the unusual vowel ordering.

Once you locate the I and E, think of words related to heat, flame, or strong emotion and consider how R and Y might fit around them.

Strategic Tip: After your opener, try guesses that test F, R and Y while locking in the IE vowel pair in the middle.

General Wordle Tips & Tricks

Want to master Wordle today and every day? Here are some timeless strategies:

  • Start Strong: Use a starting word with multiple vowels and common consonants (like ADIEU, SLATE, or RAISE).
  • Hunt for Vowels: Every word has at least one. Finding them early is key.
  • Watch for Duplicates: Letters can appear twice (e.g., ROBOT or EERIE). Don't rule this out!
