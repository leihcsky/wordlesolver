How to Play Wordle

Master the world's most popular word puzzle game with our comprehensive guide and strategies.

Game Rules

Wordle is a daily word puzzle game where you have 6 attempts to guess a 5-letter word.

Basic Rules:

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter English word
  • You have exactly 6 attempts to find the correct word
  • After each guess, the tiles change color to give you clues
  • There's only one puzzle per day, and everyone gets the same word

Winning Strategies

Best Starting Words

  • SLATE Common letters, good vowel coverage
  • ADIEU Four vowels, excellent for elimination
  • AROSE Balanced mix of vowels and consonants
  • AUDIO Four vowels, great for vowel discovery

Pro Tips

  • Start with words containing common vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
  • Use frequent consonants like R, S, T, L, N in early guesses
  • Avoid repeating letters in your first few guesses
  • Pay attention to letter frequency in English
  • Use elimination strategy - rule out impossible letters
  • Consider word patterns and common endings
