Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data and respect your privacy.

Last updated: August 29, 2025

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our Wordle Solver tool is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information
  • No Registration Required: You can use our tool without creating an account
  • Local Processing: All word solving happens in your browser
  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage patterns or game history

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal information usage:

  • Technical Data: Basic server logs for security and performance (IP addresses, browser type)
  • Anonymous Analytics: General usage statistics to improve the tool (no personal identification)
  • Error Reporting: Automatic error logs to fix bugs and improve functionality

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use advertising or tracking cookies
  • Local Storage: May store game preferences locally in your browser

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • CDN Services: For faster loading of CSS and JavaScript files
  • Web Hosting: Standard web hosting services with security measures
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
